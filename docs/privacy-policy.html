TailyDose Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

TailyDose stores pet profiles, medication schedules, dose history, and vet records locally on your device.

Data We Handle

  • Pet details you enter, such as name, species, breed, weight, vet name, vet contact, notes, medications, reminder times, and vet records.
  • Reminder metadata needed to schedule local notifications on your device.
  • Subscription status returned by Apple’s in-app purchase system.

How Data Is Used

  • To display your pets, medications, schedules, and dose history inside the app.
  • To schedule medication reminder notifications when you enable alerts.
  • To determine whether Pro subscription features are unlocked.
  • To generate export text you choose to share with your veterinarian.

Payments and Subscriptions

TailyDose does not process payment cards directly. Subscription purchases, renewals, and restores are handled by Apple through the App Store.

Data Sharing

TailyDose does not sell your personal data. The app only shares information when you explicitly use an export or share action.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, TailyDose uses reminder times and medication names to schedule local notifications on your device.

Data Retention

Your app data remains on your device until you edit or delete it, or remove the app and its local data.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, visit the support page.
