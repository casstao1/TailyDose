TailyDose Terms of Use

Last updated: April 22, 2026

TailyDose provides pet medication tracking, reminder scheduling, dose logging, and vet-ready exports.

Subscription Terms

  • Monthly: $3.99 per month
  • Annual: $29.99 per year, with a 7-day free trial when available in App Store Connect

Billing

  • Payment is charged to your Apple Account at confirmation of purchase.
  • Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.
  • Your account is charged for renewal within 24 hours before the end of the current period.
  • You can manage or cancel your subscription in your App Store account settings after purchase.

Pro Features

  • Push medication alerts
  • Multiple pets in one account
  • Vet-ready sharing and export

Medical Disclaimer

TailyDose is an organizational tool for pet care. It does not provide veterinary diagnosis or treatment advice. Always follow your veterinarian’s guidance.
